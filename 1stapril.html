Back to the roots: New Management of Global SAP Events

Newsbyte
by SAP News
April 1, 2025

WALLDORF SAP SE (NYSE: SAP) today shared that their global event management will be handed over to the ABAPConf organization team. SAP is looking into bringing back customer focus to their events.

We are looking forward to further optimizing our global event portfolio. Customers are the foundation of our success, and they deserve that SAP honours their investment and trust in our solutions said SAP CEO Christian Klein. "This new partnership allows us to offer events where customers can learn from peers and exchange lessons learned on how to get the best value from SAP solutions."

"It was a long internal process, but we came to the conclusion that we lost contact with our clients, with the people working at customers like consultants, developers, architects," said Thomas Saueressig, Customer Services & Delivery and a member of the Executive Board of SAP SE. "We are reaching CEOs, business analysts and fans, but not the ones that work with our products to deliver the value customers expect."

Driven by the success of the competition, since 2020 SAP offers their global events like Sapphire or TechEd online for free. The events provided examples of excessive usage of CGI and live streams that enabled attendees to watch the event on other platforms and to gain a better overview of alternate communications channels served by SAP. "We saw last year at ABAPConf 2024 that their professional level of providing a live stream fulfills SAP's own standards. This triggered our decision to hand the event management over to them" said Philipp Herzig, Chief Technology Officer and Chief AI Officer.

SAP hopes that the ABAPConf orga team is accepting the offer and awaits eagerly their response.
