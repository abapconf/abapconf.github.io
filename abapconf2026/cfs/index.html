Be a speaker at ABAPConf 2026!

Being a speaker at ABAPConf is the easiest way to be a speaker at an event related to SAP topics. No need to have a fancy job title or having to work with the latest and greatest. It doesn’t matter wheather you are a senior person with years of public speaking experience or a new joiner.

What we are looking for

Share your knowledge with fellow SAP developers. We are looking for talks about the real live in SAP projects at SAP customers. We are giving people that share about their daily work experience a chance to speak.

  • First and foremost: talk about what you do in your daily work
  • Main focus is on ABAP, Fiori, and SAP full stack development
    • ABAP
    • RESTful Application Programming
    • Fiori Elements & Fiori UX
  • Everything around the most important SAP language:
    • Best Practices
    • Clean Code
    • Tools & Testing

Come up with a title and an abstract.
