ABAPConf 2025 Community Day Mannheim

Welcome to the ABAPConf 2025 Community Day!


The ABAPConf 2025 Community Day is the come-together event the day before ABAPConf. Every participant traveling to Mannheim for the event is invited to stop by and to say hello. And everyone that is not participating at ABAPConf in Mannheim, but wants to say hello is also invited.

ABAPConf 2025 Community Day Mannheim

We want to provide everyone that is traveling to Mannheim and arrives a day earlier the chance to meet fellow ABAP and Fiori developers. What better to celebrate the SAP developer community than meeting other developers? The location is the same known from the HANA Tech Nights. The setup and supporting programm too: a Meetup like agenda with hand selected presentations and food and drinks.

Space is limited. We plan with max 40 people regarding seats, food and drinks.

C2: Wait, that's possible?

Mannheim 45 minutes Language: EN
A look outside the SAP bubble. How are others doing it? What can we learn from them? Or is the SAP world the best? This talk wants to discuss the topics that might influence our future as SAP developers. What should be adopt, what are we doing better?

If you look outside, you'll realise that the IT world is more than just reports. While some of the most successfull companies in the world are using SAP, the inovovation they drive has nothing to do with SAP. Be it UX, mobile, internet of things or now AI: these trends came from the outside. What does this mean for us as SAP developers? Adopt or be obsolete? Or is it them that should start listening and learn from us?
Tobias Hofmann
